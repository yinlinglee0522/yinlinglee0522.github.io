My Backstory

Born and raised in Taiwan, I am currently a first-year master student studying UX Research and Design in School of Information, University of Michigan. As a curious, enthusiastic and self-motivated explorer, I became interested in the UX field during my undergraduate years. With an information science background, I have learned how to analyze human behaviors, evaluate information systems, and investigate relationships between people and various technologies. This is where I decided to pursue more design techniques that reflect user needs, thus helping people live better and easier. With a zeal for being a professional UX Designer, I aim to create positive social impacts with human-centered methodologies.

Moreover, my obsession with art sprouting from childhood has led me to keenly observe the surroundings and cultivated my aesthetic sense. After growing up, I have continued to seize every opportunity of experiencing different perspectives of art, which is conducive to innovative ideas for designs and products.
