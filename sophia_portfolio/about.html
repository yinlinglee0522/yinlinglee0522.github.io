About

Hi, I'm Sophia Chiu

I'm currently studying my masters in Human-Computer Interaction at School of Information, University of Michigan. I was previously a Product Planner at Gogolook, taking care of our mobile app product with millions of active users across 20 countries. I treat not only my design but also myself as work-in-process that can always be improved. I first figure out the goal and purpose instead of jumping to solutions. I seek for making impact by the means of design.

I'm an UX designer with experiences of

Qualitative Research

I have done end-to-end user research including contextual inquiry, usability testing, competitive analysis.

Quantitative Research

I have employed survey, A/B testing, log analysis to verify my design hypothses in a business environment.

Cross Device Design

I have experiences of designing for a service across not only Android and iOS system but also on desktop and laptop devices.
